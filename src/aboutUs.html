We are a dynamic group of a five young professionals with a passion for web development and great travel experiences.


We believe that sharing experiences with others elevates travelling in a significant and meaningful way. Globe Trotter gives users the option of finding like-minded travellers to share their adventures with.


Our mission at Globe Trotter is to connect travellers with similar budgets, travel dates, and destinations. Users can search for similar travellers at their leisure, and can establish connections using the chat feature. Users can also maintain a personal dashboard that displays information about the trips they're currently planning, as well as about past and future trips.


We invite you to come try Globe Trotter for yourself, and see all the adventures that await you!
